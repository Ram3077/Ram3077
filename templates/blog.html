Idea Evolution of Glance.Email


A Journal of How it Started and How it is Going?

The initial Idea was to present emails in the format of News Papers. Like this...

Looked Great. Sent the image to Bala. He liked it as well. Started working on it. Everything was happening in my Laptop.

Perspective changed when I opened in my mobile. Even actual News Papers don't fit well in Mobiles.

Time to rethink...

Ideas went in the direction of charts like I do in Tableau. A donut chart of emails received by Sender. Once you click on one of the Pie (Sender), the below list will show emails only from that Sender.



As I'm writing this, I'm thinking of other options additional to the Donut chart. For example, a Word Cloud -  word frequency based distribution chart type we've used in Tableau.



Another idea is to have Siri and Alexa read through the Summary Texts. Basically, you say, 'Hey Siri, read me my emails' and Siri responds, 'Sure. Michael says 'we may need to rework on the Revenue report a bit', David says Meeting with Customer went well' and so on.

Today (19-Jul-2021), I shared this idea in the Discord Startup Channel. I never expected that conversation to explode into a different level with regard to email and other personal communication topics. It was great to see the interest in the area. It gave me confidence that I can proceed further on this idea. One user named 'Chofhe' was ready to discuss one-on-one on this.
